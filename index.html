🔥 Welcome, Firekeeper

You awaken at the dawn of humankind — a spark in the darkness. Your task is simple yet eternal: keep the fire alive. Each flame you kindle fuels civilization’s rise — from stone to stars.

Tap the fire to gather energy. Use it to unlock new eras, inventions, and the stories of humanity itself. When the flame fades, so does progress. Keep it burning, Firekeeper.
